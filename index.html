About

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Microeconomics group at University of Chicago Booth School of Business. My research focuses on collective bargaining and organized labor in Europe and the US.

Previously, I was a pre-doctoral research professional at Chicago Booth and an Associate at IDinsight. I have an MSc in Economics from the London School of Economics and Political Science, and a BA (Honors) in Economics from St. Stephen's College, University of Delhi.
